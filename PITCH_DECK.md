## Slide 2: Problem
**The Challenge:**  
- Slow incident reporting delays emergency response
- Limited real-time communication between citizens, responders, and authorities
- No centralized system for tracking and managing incidents
- Lack of visibility into responder locations and availability

---

## Slide 3: Solution
**SmartOps - The All-in-One Platform:**  
This smart incident reporting platform connects citizens, responders, and administrators through real-time communication, enabling rapid incident reporting, efficient response coordination, and comprehensive system management with analytics and live updates.

## Slide 5: Key Features

| User Role | Key Features |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Citizen** | Report incidents with media & location, track status in real time |
| **Responder** | View assigned tasks, share live location, update incident status |
| **Admin** | Analytics dashboard, manage users & incidents, approve responders |

**Common Features:**  
Real-time notifications, responsive design, WebSocket updates, secure authentication

---

## Slide 6: Product Demo Highlights
1. **Rapid Reporting:** Citizen reports incident with photo & GPS location in < 30 seconds
2. **Live Coordination:** Admin assigns nearest responder instantly
3. **Status Updates:** Responder updates status from "assigned" → "in progress" → "resolved"
4. **Analytics Dashboard:** Real-time metrics on incidents, response times, and active responders
